Article and Aside

The article tag can be used to contain content like a newspaper article. Other examples of are movie reviews, case studies,a product description, etc. You can nest articles as long as they contain related content. A webpage can have many article tags. Sometimes you may want to add a little textbox on the sidebar containing links or a quote or many other options, this would be done as an aside. It is common to think of an aside as a sidebar. It can be a sidebar, but you can place an aside element in a variety of places in your webpage. You can apply CSS styles to have the aside appear alongside the main content instead of below it. You can nest it within the primary content of your page. In my opinion, it is more astetically pleasing as a sticky note style sidebar on the webpage.
